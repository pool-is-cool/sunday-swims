AIS vessel tracking — canal Anderlecht
How AIS works: Commercial vessels are required by law to broadcast an AIS (Automatic Identification System) signal containing their position, speed, heading, name and vessel type. This allows real-time tracking of large ships on the canal. The data shown here is collected from AIS receivers near the swimming zone.
